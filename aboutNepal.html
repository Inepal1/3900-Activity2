Brief journey through Nepal

Nepal is a small country in South Asia, famous for its beautiful mountains and rich culture. It is home to Mount Everest, the tallest mountain in the world. Nepal has amazing views, old temples, and unique traditions. It is known for its peaceful environment and warm, welcoming people.

Major Attractions:

  • kathmandu City
  • Pokhara
  • Lumbini-The Birth Place of Gautam Buddha
  • Annapurna Basecamp Trek
